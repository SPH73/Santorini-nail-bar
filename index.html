Meet your nail designer

Samantha Saloman is naturally artistic and has been creating things since she was very young; anything from paintings to jewellery. She began experimenting with her own nails at the age of 15, quickly becoming so good that she started getting requests from her friends and family members to do theirs.

After finishing school, Samantha went on to study Somatology and thereafter added microblading to her growing repertoire. Her passion for nail design pushed her to obtain a formal qualification and she now focuses her time on creating unique designs for her clients, continuously honing her craft and keeping abreast of the latest international trends.
